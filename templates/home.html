Home

The Muslim Students' Association (MSA) at Memorial University is a student-based organization under the Memorial Students' Union that was established in 1976. Since then, the MSA strives to serve Allah (God) in the best way possible through the principles laid out in the Honorable Quran and Teachings of Beloved Prophet Muhammed (Peace and Blessings Be Upon Him). We seek to achieve this goal by promoting unity, providing education, and giving back to the local community through a wide array of activities held each year. We hope that this website provides a means of giving current and prospective students more information about the MSA, our events, activities, services, and some general information about Memorial University.
